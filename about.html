About Lord Tsarcasm

My Journey

Welcome to my creative corner of the internet, where AI-music, human music, AI and human art, and video production intersect to bring the world of R.O.D. (Remnants of Destruction) to life. Inspired by the incredible legacy of my late friend, Sean Trusty, R.O.D. is more than just a passion project—it's a world rich with lore, characters, and stories that I am committed to developing into a fully immersive video game. Maybe one day :) As the creative force behind this endeavor, I combine my talents with words with AI and human music composition, AI-driven artwork, and cinematic storytelling to expand and explore the universe Sean began. Alongside several collaborators, I am craft a narrative experience that honors Sean's vision while pushing the boundaries of what's possible in interactive media. What was once a journey of self-discovery has finally found focus in video games. So be it :) Here, you'll find my work, including the music of Lord Tsarcasm—a storyteller bard band that narrates the epic tales of R.O.D.—as well as AI-generated art and videos that breathe life into the characters and settings of this expansive world. Join me on this journey as we continue to build and share the world of R.O.D., one story at a time.

What I'm working on
