About Use The Knife

Use The Knife is a post-apocalyptic fantasy RPG that combines narrative-driven gameplay with an expansive world rich in history, danger, and mystical elements. Built with flexibility and creativity in mind, this game allows you to explore a world where survival is just the beginning.

Players can immerse themselves in a dynamic world, where every decision impacts the future of the desolate wastelands. The choices you make, the alliances you form, and the paths you take all shape the outcome of your story.
