Lord Tsarcasm is the enigmatic alter ego of Xris Hawkins, a solitary NPC psionicist within the world of ROD. Together, they embody both a masterful storyteller and a rare, god-like presence, seamlessly weaving intricate narratives and occasionally intervening in player campaigns with their formidable powers. As Lord Tsarcasm, Xris Hawkins guides adventurers through epic tales, shaping destinies and adding depth to the unfolding saga with each subtle yet impactful intervention.
